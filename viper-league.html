Viper League

I spent 6 week working as part of a team of interns at You.i TV to create Viper League, a soccer streaming app. I worked with Ashley Murray and Kyle Jackson to bring this design from concept to a functional application. We were tasked to create an app for You.i TV's Engine One, a highly customizeable app running off of their engine. We created the brand, designed the app and brought it to life.

This was a great experience and I really learned a lot about working with a team and in a proffesional space and workflow.
