Plumbus Poster

This was a project from design school, to design an event poster. It could be a concert, movie, product launch, etc. I chose a product launch, and one that everyone has in their home, the Plumbus.

This simple yet unique design from the minds of the Rick and Morty creators worked perfectly for this job, which required the use of Illustrators hit-and-miss image trace feature.
