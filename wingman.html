Wingman

Wingman is an app designed to work with a wearable smart product. Paired with a smartwatch, it helps you track your Blood Alcohol Concentration (BAC).

Using the sub-dermal scanner on the back of your smartwatch, Wingman can calculate your BAC by analysing your sweat. It was designed to provide awareness to ensure users can get home safely.
