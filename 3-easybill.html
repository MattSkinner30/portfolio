PillPal App Design

Easybill is an app that is meant to help pay for and divide bills at a restaurant.

Through Easybill, you can easily divide up the ordered items between multiple people, decide on a tip and pay on your own time. Using a simple drag and drop system, picking which items everyone will pay for is simpler than ever.
