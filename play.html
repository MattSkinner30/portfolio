5 Second Animation - Play

The challenge of this project was to create a 5 second animation themed around "play".

Rotoscopeing is based on footage, where certain elements are drawn frame by frame to give a hand drawn look, yet have realistic motion. Speeing up the original footage to only a second allowed me to create this rapid fire animation.
